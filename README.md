Hej! This repository will be used to collaborate on analyses during our next session.

We are using data about mental health and connected with nature among participants who do and do not own an allotment. 
The data are from this paper: Data on Connection With the Natural Environment and Its Impact on Mental Health Among Allotment and Non-Allotment Owners, Haywood and Stiller (2024)
https://doi.org/10.5334/jopd.122
